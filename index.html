Note

  • First person in the Attendance File is considered as Organizer. Meeting starts when Organizer Joins. That's how Teams work.!
  • The Meeting End time is used to calculate the Duration if the Attendee never left the Meeting.
  • If an Attendee left and rejoined the Meeting, total duration will be calculated by his/her consolidated duration of time present in the Meeting.
